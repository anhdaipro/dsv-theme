Responsibilities

  • Develop wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs for web and mobile applications.
  • Collaborate with business analyst, and stakeholders to understand user needs, design opportunities, and business goals.
  • Work closely with development teams to ensure designs are implemented accurately and efficiently.
  • Conduct user research and testing to validate design concepts and iterate on designs.
  • Create and maintain design systems, style guides, and UI components.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in UI/UX design.

Your skills and experiences

  • At least over 2 year working years in UI/UX design experience in web and mobile applications.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing UI/UX design work.
  • Proficiency in design tools: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite or similar.
  • Ability to create responsive designs that work across multiple devices and platforms.
  • Good communication skills and ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Self-motivated and ability to work independently with minimal supervision
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum development methodologies is a plus.
  • Proficiency in English at a basic level is necessary.
